"Glass Luck Club" - The Vision

The Glass Luck Club is a community of app developers for Google Glass. We teach and learn from each other, we share our code and expertise, and we collaborate toward the common goal, that is, making Google Glass a reality. (The phrase "glass luck" is a pun on the word "potluck", and we have tentatively chosen this name to emphasize the spirit of sharing.) Please provide your email address below, and we will keep you posted on our progress. "Signing up" here does not obligate you in any way to join our "club". We will, however, provide substantial benefits to people who sign up early, including preferential pricing (that is, if we decide to make this a paid community). Coming soon...
